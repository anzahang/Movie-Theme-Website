Welcome!

 

The Harry Potter universe has captured and constructed the childhoods of many. It can be argued that the books were the better among the books and the movies, but the movies were a exceptionally well adapted and interpreted image of where the books took our imagination. Although the Harry Potter universe has aged, it will remain a timeless masterpiece. With a cast of characters and atmosphere that depicts and corresponds well to the intended imagination of its creator, The Harry Potter Movies will be a remarkable new experience for many and a nostalgic legacy for many more.

There are a total of 8 Harry Potter Movies with each movie correlating to one book, with the exception of the last book, "The Deathly Hallows", being made into two movies. The list of movies are as follows:

  • Harry Potter And The Philosopher's Stone

  • Harry Potter And The Chamber Of Secrets

  • Harry Potter And The Prisoner Of Azkaban

  • Harry Potter And The Goblet Of Fire

  • Harry Potter And The Order Of The Phoenix 

  • Harry Potter And The Half-Blood Prince

  • Harry Potter And The Deathly Hallows I

  • Harry Potter And The Deathly Hallows II
